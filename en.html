Research Overview

Katsuya Tanaka
Professor, Faculty of Economics / Research Center for Sustainability and Environment / Data Science & AI Innovation Center, Shiga University
Global Senior Fellow, Portland State University (USA)
Global Fellow, Ewha Womans University (Korea)

We advance data-driven solutions for sustainable urban environments. Our current focus spans four areas:

  1. Disaster preparedness and urban resilience
  2. Green infrastructure for risk reduction and climate adaptation
  3. Smart mobility (shared streets, public transport + cycling, ride-sharing)
  4. Regional revitalization through the three pillars above

We welcome motivated undergraduate and graduate students interested in empirical analysis for real-world problems.
