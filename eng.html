Katsuya Tanaka

Professor, Shiga University / Environmental Research Center / Data Science & AI Research Promotion Center

Global Senior Fellow, Portland State University · Global Fellow, Ewha Womans University

I study how data-driven policy and community design can build resilient, low-carbon, and socially connected cities. My work blends environmental economics, disaster information science, and urban planning to inform practical policy.

Highlights

  • Working across disaster preparedness, green infrastructure, and smart mobility.
  • Lead author and collaborator on peer‑reviewed papers in IJDRR, JEM, Sustainability, The Gerontologist, and more.
  • Developer of the FactorialSurvey R package for factorial survey experiments (FSE).

Research Focus

  • Preparedness for disasters and urban resilience
  • Green infrastructure for risk reduction and heat adaptation
  • Smart mobility: shared streets, public transit + bicycling, rideshare
  • Community revitalization through the above
